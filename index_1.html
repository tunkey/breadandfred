«Bread & Fred: Climb the crest» was born from watching streamers and youtubers playing «rage games». We had very clear the type of game we wanted to make and the penguin theme because, who doesn’t like a penguin game? But to add a twist to the genre, to give the game an original touch … We thought about making it cooperative! Two players will have to put themselves in the shoes of Bread and Fred and try to climb the mountain tied by a rope, work together, fall many times, get angry and, probably, break their relationship.

Do you think you are good enough at climbing and overcoming all obstacles? You can also time your climb with Speedrun Mode activated, and brag about your climb time.

Call a friend and go on this adventure! Play, have fun, overcome the challenges, fall, rage, do the clapping animation at the right moment … The real question is: Can you climb to the top?
